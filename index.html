your goal is to make a mathematical expression that equals 10 using four numbers in order. allowed characters: +-*/().^!.

technical notes
  • not all puzzles are solvable
  • the calculator is an edited version of math-expression-evaluator.
  • ^ uses JS Math.pow() which has some unintended behavior with 0, NaN, and Infinity (namely, 0^0 = 1)
